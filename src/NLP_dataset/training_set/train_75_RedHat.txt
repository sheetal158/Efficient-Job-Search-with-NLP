Job summary

The Red Hat Engineering team is seeking a continuous integration developer to join us as a Software Engineer, ideally in Westford, MA, but other locations will be considered for the right candidate. In this role, you'll join the globally distributed Red Hat Enterprise Linux Virtualization team to develop and maintain a continuous integration environment for the Kernel-based Virtual Machine (KVM), a core technology of Red Hat Enterprise Linux (RHEL). You will help us maintain our exceptional quality standards by developing and maintaining a continuous integration infrastructure and providing support to the team. You'll develop and strengthen the depth and breadth of continuous integration testing for KVM, as well as promote continuous integration infrastructure improvements to enhance software quality, stability, reporting capabilities, and user experience. This position is a great opportunity to work alongside some of the brightest engineers in the open source industry. We'll need you to have experience with continuous integration technologies, open source software development, and either quality assurance or release engineering.
Primary job responsibilities

    Ensure maintenance and reliability of the continuous integration environment
    Facilitate, design, and implement continuous integration improvements
    Maintain continuous integration documentation, like environment diagrams, workflows, and processes
    Assist and support downstream maintainer activities

Required skills

    Proven Python, shell, or other scripting expertise; C or Python programming experience is a plus
    Experience in continuous integration
    Expertise with testing frameworks (Jenkins, AutoTest, or equivalent)
    Specific Virt-Test or Avocado experience is a plus
    Ability to develop and implement test plans
    Experience with or knowledge of OpenStack, Red Hat Enterprise Virtualization (RHEV), or QEMU is an advantage
    Experience contributing to open source projects is an advantage
    Familiarity with X86, PPC, or ARM64 architectures is a plus
    Familiarity with iSCSI, Fiber Channel, or other storage technologies is a plus
    Ability to work independently in a globally distributed environment
    Good communication skills
