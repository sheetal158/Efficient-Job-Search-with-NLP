Division Description
The Siemens Digital Factory Division offers a comprehensive portfolio of seamlessly integrated hardware, software and technology-based services in order to support manufacturing companies worldwide in enhancing the flexibility and efficiency of their manufacturing processes and reducing the time to market of their products.

For more information, please visit: http://www.siemens.com/businesses/us/en/digital-factory.htm


Job Description
Duties and Responsibilities:

Coordinate development of the integration between the SES products and Teamcenter
Provide technical ownership and oversight of the project
Create and maintain architectural deliverables such as Data Models and Use Cases
Analyze and resolve technical issues and evaluate design alternatives
Collaborate closely with other architects and the product team to deliver a solution that meets the market needs
Ensure customer use cases are factored into the architectural evolution of the solution

Minimum Requirements:

Multiple years of experience working within the Teamcenter environment
Experience working with NX Manager or Teamcenter integration with Catia
Background in software engineering and at least 5-10 years of professional experience in a software architect role.
Strong knowledge of C++ or Java
Experience working with Eclipse, Web based applications and SOA development
Demonstrated leadership skills; ability to work independently with minimal supervision
Excellent written and verbal communication skills
